BibleSmarts Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how BibleSmarts collects, uses, shares, and secures your information.

We Do Not Collect Personal Data

BibleSmarts does not collect any personal information from you. This includes but is not limited to your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other data that could be used to identify you.

Ad Serving

BibleSmarts uses third-party advertising technologies to serve ads. These third-party companies may collect non-personal information about your use of the app to provide you with interest-based advertising. This information may include but is not limited to your device type, operating system, and app usage.

For more information about how these third-party ad networks collect and use your information, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
